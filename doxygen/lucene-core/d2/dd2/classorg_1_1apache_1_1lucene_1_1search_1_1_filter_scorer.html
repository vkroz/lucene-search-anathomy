Detailed Description

A.

FilterScorer

contains another

Scorer

, which it uses as its basic source of data, possibly transforming the data along the way or providing additional functionality. The class

FilterScorer

itself simply implements all abstract methods of

Scorer

with versions that pass all requests to the contained scorer. Subclasses of

FilterScorer

may further override some of these methods and may also provide additional methods and fields.
