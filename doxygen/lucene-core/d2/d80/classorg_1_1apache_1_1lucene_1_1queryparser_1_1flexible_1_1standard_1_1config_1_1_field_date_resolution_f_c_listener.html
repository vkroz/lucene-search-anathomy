Detailed Description

This listener listens for every field configuration request and assign a ConfigurationKeys#DATE_RESOLUTION to the equivalent FieldConfig based on a defined map: fieldName -> Resolution stored in ConfigurationKeys#FIELD_DATE_RESOLUTION_MAP.
