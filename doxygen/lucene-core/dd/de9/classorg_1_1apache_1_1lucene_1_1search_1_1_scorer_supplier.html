A supplier of Scorer.

This allows to get an estimate of the cost before building the Scorer.

Parameters
leadCostCost of the scorer that will be used in order to lead iteration. This can be interpreted as an upper bound of the number of times that DocIdSetIterator#nextDoc, DocIdSetIterator#advance and TwoPhaseIterator#matches will be called. Under doubt, pass Long#MAX_VALUE, which will produce a Scorer that has good iteration capabilities.
