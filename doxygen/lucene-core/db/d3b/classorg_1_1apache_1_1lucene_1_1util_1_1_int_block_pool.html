◆ nextBuffer()

void org.apache.lucene.util.IntBlockPool.nextBuffer ( )

Advances the pool to its next buffer.

This method should be called once after the constructor to initialize the pool. In contrast to the constructor a IntBlockPool#reset() call will advance the pool to its first buffer immediately.
